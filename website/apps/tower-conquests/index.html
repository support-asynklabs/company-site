About the App

Take over the battlefield in Tower Strategy War — a fast-paced, real-time strategy game where every tower you capture brings you closer to victory.

  • Real-time troop dispatch — draw lanes, send waves, cut supply lines
  • Three tower types including golden Cavalry and neutral towers
  • In-battle power-ups: Bomb, Heal, and 2× troop generation boost
  • Earn coins for every win — no paywalls, just smart strategy
