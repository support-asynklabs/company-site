Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last updated: May 12, 2026

At AsynkLabs, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our websites, mobile applications, Play games, and related services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, user account details, and payment-related information such as UPI ID when required for rewards or withdrawals.
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, device information, operating system, app version, gameplay activity, session duration, pages visited, and referring URLs.
  • Advertising Data: Advertising identifiers, device identifiers, interaction with ads, and reward-related ad activity.
  • Communication Data: Messages or inquiries sent via email, forms, or support channels.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve our applications and services.
  • Track gameplay, scores, rewards, and leaderboard activities.
  • Process withdrawals, rewards, or redemption requests.
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, fake traffic, emulator usage, and cheating.
  • Show advertisements and measure ad performance.
  • Analyse website and app performance to improve user experience.
  • Respond to support requests and inquiries.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

3. Play Games Reward System

Our applications may include reward systems such as coins, points, leaderboard rewards or virtual items based on gameplay or app activities.

  • Rewards may require verification before approval.
  • We reserve the right to reject rewards associated with fraud, fake accounts, VPN abuse, emulator usage, automated activity, ad abuse, or suspicious behaviour.
  • Multiple accounts created for unfair advantage may be suspended or permanently banned.
  • Reward values, eligibility, redemption rules, and availability may change without notice.

4. Advertisements & Analytics

Our apps and websites may display banner ads, interstitial ads, rewarded ads, and other advertising formats.

We may use third-party advertising and analytics providers including:

  • Google AdMob
  • Google Firebase
  • AppLovin
  • Unity Ads
  • Meta Audience Network
  • Other mediation and analytics providers

These providers may collect advertising identifiers, device information, usage data, and approximate location data to personalise ads, prevent fraud, and measure advertising performance.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website and applications may use cookies, SDKs, pixels, and similar tracking technologies to improve functionality, analyse traffic, personalise content, and measure advertisement performance.

You can control certain tracking preferences through your browser or device settings.

6. Permissions

Depending on app functionality, we may request permissions such as:

  • Internet access
  • Notification access
  • Storage access
  • Camera access
  • Phone or device state information

Permissions are requested only when required for app functionality.

7. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With trusted service providers, cloud platforms, analytics providers, advertising partners, or payment processors assisting our services.
  • When required by law, regulation, or legal process.
  • To protect the rights, safety, security, or integrity of AsynkLabs, our users, or others.

8. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary for operational, legal, security, fraud prevention, and business purposes. Data may be securely deleted or anonymised when no longer required.

9. Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organisational security measures to protect your information from unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Fraud Prevention & Fair Usage

To maintain fair gameplay and reward distribution, we may monitor gameplay patterns, IP addresses, device identifiers, emulator usage, and suspicious activities.

Accounts involved in cheating, fake traffic generation, automated behaviour, reward abuse, or ad abuse may be restricted, suspended, or permanently terminated.

11. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Withdraw consent where applicable.
  • Lodge complaints with relevant data protection authorities.

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@asynklabs.com.

12. Third-Party Links & Services

Our services may contain links or integrations with third-party websites, payment systems, analytics tools, or advertising providers. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services.

13. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.
